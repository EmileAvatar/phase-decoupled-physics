## Movement Descriptions

The following observations recur across multiple documents. All are witness
accounts — no instrument-measured velocities, accelerations, or energy
signatures were included in this batch.

| Incident | Location / date | Observed movement | Physics note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Military pilot accounts (multiple) | Various, unspecified | Descended from altitude, hovered motionless against wind, then accelerated "at rates no human pilot could survive" | Instantaneous transition from stationary to extreme acceleration with no visible thrust signature |
| Super-hot orb | Unspecified, 2025 | Hovered stationary, then outpaced pursuing helicopter over ~20 miles | Abrupt onset of velocity from rest; no deceleration signature reported |
| Linear luminescent object | Drone operator report, Sep 2023 | Appeared, held position for 5–10 s, vanished — no deceleration | Disappearance without braking |
| Unidentified object | Kazakhstan, date unspecified | Executed "90-degree turns" at speed | Direction change with no loss of speed — implies near-zero inertial reaction |
| Football-shaped object | Near Japan, 2024 (US Indo-Pacific Command) | No movement detail reported | Shape only |

**Common pattern across accounts:** objects appear to have zero (or near-zero)
coupling to inertia during acceleration and turns. No sonic boom reported for
any supersonic event. Hovering against wind/gravity with no visible thrust.
