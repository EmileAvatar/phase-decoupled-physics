# Phase-Decoupled Transport Platform (PDTP)

A conceptual vehicle that moves by reducing and shaping its phase coupling
to spacetime rather than using thrust.

---

## Core Principle

Gravity is interpreted as:

Phase-locking between matter-waves and spacetime-waves.

Inertia arises from the same coupling.

By weakening this lock:

- Gravity decreases
- Inertia decreases

---

## PDTP Definition

A Phase-Decoupled Transport Platform:

- Maintains highly coherent internal matter-waves
- Actively controls internal phase alignment
- Alters coupling strength with spacetime

The vehicle does not push against air or space.
