# Refinement Update: Risk Adjustments & Precision Improvements

This section strengthens terminology to reduce misinterpretation while preserving pedagogical clarity.

## 1. Naming Adjustments for Greater Accuracy

### Gravity

**Previous:**  
Gravity → Mass Attraction

**Improved:**  
Gravity → Spacetime Curvature Rule  
or  
Gravity → Mass–Energy Coupling Rule

**Why**
- Gravity is not simply attraction.
- In General Relativity, mass-energy curves spacetime.
- Objects follow curved geodesics, not a pulling force.
- This preserves compatibility with Einstein’s equations.

---

### Quantum Fields

**Previous:**  
Quantum Field → Particle Generator

**Improved:**  
Quantum Field → Excitation Substrate  
or  
Quantum Field → Distributed Dynamic System

**Why**
- Fields do not “generate” particles like factories.
- Particles are excitations of pre-existing fields.
- Fields carry energy, momentum, and can propagate waves.
- This preserves physical realism.

---

### Fields (General)

**Previous:**  
Fields → Constraints + Permissions

**Improved:**  
Fields → Distributed Dynamic Systems

**Why**
- Fields are active physical entities.
- They evolve according to equations of motion.
- They are not passive rule-sets.
- This avoids under-representing their dynamical role.

---

### Dark Energy

**Previous:**  
Expansion Driver

**Improved:**  
Metric Expansion Bias  
or  
Spacetime Background Pressure

**Why**
- Dark energy affects the metric expansion of spacetime.
- It behaves like a constant energy density.
- The new name better reflects its role in cosmology.
