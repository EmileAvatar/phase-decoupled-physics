PDTP: Spacetime & Matter

Visualizing the Multi-Medium Picture and Emergent Gravity. Spacetime is not an empty void, but a fluid of pre-quantized grains. Matter is not a fluid, but localized "clocks" riding the medium.

Gravitational Condensate (Medium Clocks, $\phi$)
The background grid. Each point is a grain of the medium with its own phase (represented by rotating lines/colors).
Matter (Matter Clocks, $\psi$)
The glowing orbs. Individual wave/clocks traveling through space. e.g., Atoms, quarks, electrons.
Phase-Locking (Gravity)
Lines show $\cos(\psi - \phi)$ coupling. Matter synchronizes with local spacetime grains, creating a "wake" of curved spacetime (gravity).
